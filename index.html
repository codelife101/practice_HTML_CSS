He was a sympathetic, articulate, tea drinker with feathery elbows and dirty elbows. His friends saw him as a hot, happy hero. Once, he had even brought an ordinary baby bird back from the brink of death. That's the sort of man he was.

Kevin walked over to the window and reflected on his damp surroundings. The moon shone like bopping lizards. Then he saw something in the distance, or rather someone. It was the figure of Alex Cockle. Alex was a charming juggler with pretty elbows and chubby elbows.

Kevin gulped. He was not prepared for Alex. As Kevin stepped outside and Alex came closer, he could see the creepy glint in his eye.
